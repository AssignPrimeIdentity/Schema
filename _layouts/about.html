Concept Nature

In short, our concept is all about formation. An easy example, counting the total number of an individual duplicated in pair will become three. Pairing it to even with another one, the total will be seven (7).
Forming the seven to three by four lies twelve. Pairing them to even produces a total of twenty four (24).
In our concept these process would present total hours and days that stays fix in a cycle (24/7).

The concept covers also the nature of life object. In humans, each cell normally contains twenty-three pairs of chromosomes, for a total of forty-six. Twenty-two of these pairs, called autosomes, look the same in both males and females. The last pair, the sex chromosomes, differ between males and females. Females have two copies of the X chromosome, while males have one X and one Y chromosome.
In our concept, the twenty-two with the other two produces a total number of twenty four (24).

Going further into the process of DNA Replication in a chromosome, you may find a conjunction with the routes on all processes that happen in our mapping system. As our mapping is generated in seven (7) kind of depth processes, where, at last, the route generate their channels in hexagonal form that coherence with six to six even twelve to twelve like the hours in cycle (24). So when the concept is implemented by an individual as a project then it will form unified like so time cycle (24/7).
